Bianca's Movies List

My top 3 Movies

Spirited Away

Visual poetry comes together in the movie Spirited Away, with wonderous colors and characters in a world mimicking our own, truly a feat from the mind of the great Hayao Miyazaki.

The Love Witch

The beautiful cinematography is reminiscent of a bygone era, with strange and magical elements coming together to create a truly memorable viewing experience.

Call me by your name

"Is it better to speak or die?", is a beautiful question posed by Call Me By Your Name, it perfectly captures the feelings and nuance of a first love.
